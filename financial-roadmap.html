Portfolio Construction & Risk Management
Markowitz · Rebalancing · Currency Risk Overlay · Position Sizing
Wks 7–10 · ~24 hrs High Value ▼
Why This Matters For You
You already invest in both BIST and S&P 500 — but do you have a systematic allocation framework? Most investors have implicit portfolios they never explicitly designed. Your OR background in optimization makes portfolio theory feel like familiar territory: mean-variance optimization is literally a quadratic programming problem.
🔬 OR Direct Application: Markowitz optimization = minimize σ²(Rp) subject to E(Rp) ≥ target and Σwᵢ = 1. This is a QP problem. You can solve this with scipy.optimize.minimize() or CVXPY right now with your Python skills.
🇹🇷 Unique Challenge: Your human capital (salary from Turkish banking) correlates with Turkish macro risk. Your BIST holdings add more Turkey exposure on top. True portfolio thinking requires treating your future salary stream as an asset in the optimization — and realizing you may be massively over-exposed to Turkey risk in total.
